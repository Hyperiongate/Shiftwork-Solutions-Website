Challenges We Solve
Sound Familiar?
These are the problems that bring operations leaders to our door. Every one of them is solvable with the right schedule and the right process.
⏲

Overtime is out of control

Chronic overtime drives up costs and burns out your best people. The 20/60/20 rule applies: 20% of your workforce wants every hour you can offer, 60% will do their share, and 20% wants none. The schedule should reflect that reality.

🚪

You cannot keep people

Turnover is rarely about pay alone. When employees feel unheard about their schedules, they leave for competitors who offer predictability. A workforce that helps choose its schedule stays.

🕑

You need to go 24/7

The leap from 5-day to 7-day continuous operations is the biggest workforce change most facilities will ever face. Employees fear the unknown. Done right, most end up preferring the new schedule.

📈

Coverage gaps are hurting output

Absenteeism, skill imbalances, and poorly designed shift overlaps leave machines idle and supervisors scrambling. Schedule design is an engineering problem — it deserves an engineering solution.

🗣

A schedule change went badly before

Many facilities have tried to change schedules and failed — or succeeded technically but lost trust. Change management is not a PowerPoint. It is walking the shop floor and earning buy-in, crew by crew.

📊

You do not know what your workforce wants

Assumptions about employee preferences are wrong more often than right. A structured survey with normative benchmarking reveals what your people actually think — and how they compare to similar operations.

Our Process
How We Work
A typical engagement runs about six weeks. We do the heavy lifting. You keep running your operation.
1

Discovery & Business Analysis

We learn your operation: coverage needs, cost structure, workforce demographics, constraints, and goals. We review your data, walk your facility, and talk to your people.

2

Employee Survey & Engagement

A comprehensive workforce survey reveals what employees actually want — schedule preferences, overtime appetite, work-life balance priorities. We benchmark results against industry norms.

3

Schedule Design & Modeling

We design schedule options that meet your business requirements and align with workforce preferences. Each option comes with full cost modeling and coverage analysis.

4

Employee Choice & Implementation

Employees review the options and choose. We build the implementation manual, communication plan, and policy framework. The schedule they pick becomes their schedule — not management's.
