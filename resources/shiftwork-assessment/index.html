How It Works
A short exercise in what’s knowable — and what isn’t.

Every shift operations leader makes daily decisions based on what they believe about their workforce — who wants overtime, who doesn’t, what employees value, how they feel about the schedule they’re working.

Some of those beliefs are accurate. Some aren’t. The tricky part is knowing which is which.

This short exercise lets you compare your instincts to patterns drawn from our benchmark database — more than 20,000 shift worker responses across hundreds of 24/7 operations. You’ll see where your thinking aligns with what shift workers across North America actually say, and, just as importantly, where the honest answer for your specific facility remains an open question.

About the Benchmark

The Shiftwork Solutions Normative Database

The comparisons and analysis you’ve seen are drawn from the Shiftwork Solutions normative database — responses from more than 20,000 shift workers across hundreds of 24/7 operations in 16 industries. It’s the largest benchmark dataset of its kind, built over three decades of client engagements. The full dataset isn’t publicly available; it’s used in our workforce surveys and schedule design engagements, where it becomes the foundation for understanding how a specific workforce compares to the industry as a whole.
